Our Services

What we are best at - expert irrigation

System Maintenance

Yes your neighbors grass is greener than yours, because they had their system Maintenance. There is no need to have lawn envy, allow our team of professionals to take care of your Hunter, Rainbird, or Toro system!

Controller Upgrade

Looking to upgrade that old controller? Hunter Hydrawise Smart Controllers are a nice option! Available in 6, 12, and 24 zones. Smart controllers only water when your lawn needs it! Call us for pricing 508-239-2033

Installations

Looking to give your lawn the much needed sprinklet system it deserves? Look no further than Robert Rosa. We offer competitive rates on all installations and can recommend the best system for your lawn.

Start ups

Existing systems need to be started up after a hard winter, this spring let us come and get your lawn ready for the season.

Winterizing Systems

Our winterization experts can take care of all your shut down, blowout, draining, insulating, and winterizing needs. If you had a summer installation make sure that you keep it safe through winterization.

Trouble shooting

A yearly checkup is important to maintain your system effectively. If you're having issues with your system make sure to call us, our team will tell you exactly what you're dealing with and the best next steps to solve it!
